🚚 Shipping rates & info
Standard flat-rate shipping is $4.99 (5-7 business days). Express shipping is available for $14.99 (2-3 business days). We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Currently, we ship only to the United States and Canada; international shipping to locations like India is unavailable.
💳 Compare Service Tiers
Standard Tier: Access standard shipping rates, online self-service support.
Premium Tier: Priority support with < 2 hour response window, 10% discount on shipping.
Enterprise Tier: Dedicated support agent, custom integration options, negotiated rates.
📦 Returns & Shipping Fees
Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase in original condition. Return shipping fees are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective or incorrect. We charge a flat $5.99 return processing fee.
🕒 Business Hours & Phone
Our customer support phone line (1-800-555-0199) is open Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. Online chat support is available 24/7.
